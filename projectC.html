User Instruction

1.View Control

Drag mouse to aim a point, use 'Z' to move forward and 'X' to move backward. Press left and right arrow keys to slide sideways left/right; Press up and down arrow keys to move up and down.

2.Mode Control

There are 10 modes which can be switched to by pressing corresponding number keys:
Press key '1' to switch to Phong lighting with Gouraud Shading mode;
Press key '2' to switch to Blinn-Phong lighting with Gouraud Shading mode;
Press key '3' to switch to Phong lighting with Phong Shading mode;
Press key '4' to switch to Blinn-Phong lighting with Phong Shading mode;
Press key '5' to switch to Cook-Torrance Shader mode;
Press key '6' to switch to Texture mode;
Press key '7' to switch to None distance dependencies mode;
Press key '8' to switch to distance dependencies mode with 1/dist;
Press key '9' to switch to distance dependencies mode with 1/dist2;
Press key '0' to switch to Distortion mode;
Press 'G' to change to a view point that is easy to compare different modes.

3.Light source Control

There are two light sources in my work. Light source 1 is a headlight source, light source 2 is initially on the top of z axis.
Key 'T' is used like the key 'shift' in my work.
Press 'J' to move light source 2 along -x direction and press 'T'+'J' to move light source 2 along +x direction;
Press 'L' to move light source 2 along -y direction and press 'T'+'L' to move light source 2 along +y direction;
Press 'K' to move light source 2 along -z direction and press 'T'+'K' to move light source 2 along +z direction;
Press 'U' to turn on/off ambient term of light source 1, Press 'I' to turn on/off diffuse term of light source 1, Press 'O' to turn on/off specular term of light source 1;
Press 'B' to turn on/off ambient term of light source 2, Press 'N' to turn on/off diffuse term of light source 2, Press 'M' to turn on/off specular term of light source 2;
Use form below to adjust parameters of ambient,diffuse and specular parts of light source 2;

4.Flying-airplane navigation controls

Press 'C' to make velocity smaller and 'T'+'C' to make velocity larger. Check console for current velocity.
Press 'V' to make camera roll counter clockwise and 'T'+'V' to make camera roll clockwise.

5.Trackball control

Press 'H' and drag mouse to make the clock rotate. If you don't press 'H' the viewpoint will also rotate.

6.Adjustable joints

Press 'P' to make the rotation angle of hand larger and 'T'+'P' to make rotation angle smaller.
Press 'Y' to make the rotation angle of finger larger and 'T'+'Y' to make rotation angle smaller.

7.Miscellaneous

Press Q and E to change the size of robot and R and F to change the size of the clock.
Press W,A,S,D to change the robot's leg. Click to change the position of the robot.

Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
